Killing Free Time

I am actually quite laid back when it comes to free time, I don't really do a whole lot. When I have free time I like to...

  • Play Video Games
  • Watch Youtube
  • Watch the Red Sox/Patriots Games
  • Hang out with friends
  • Go on dates with my girlfriend
  • Enjoy alone time
  • Go to the movies

It doesn't take to much to entertain me, I'm usually either just playing some video games, watching something that I find interesting, or I'm somewhere in Bangor, Maine doing the same things over and over again. Sometimes I do enjoy going somewhere that I usually don't go a lot however. I don't mind taking a day trip to Bar Harbor, Maine or Portland, Maine because they have so much more to do than we do around central Maine. However out of all the things I do during my free time I mostly spend that with my girlfriend because it's my favorite thing to do. It doesn't matter what we are doing, as long as we are together and that's all I need in life, just somebody to make me laugh and have a good time with.
